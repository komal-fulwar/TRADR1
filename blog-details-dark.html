What is the TRADR MENTORSHIP PROGRAM?

It is an in-person training program to learn stock market trading from basics to advance.

What does ONE ON ONE training mean?

It means that there will be face to face physical classes. It is not any internet course.

Why only 5 members?

We at TRADR believe that trading is a skill where personal attention is required. With only 5 members in the batch, we will focus on each one of you and clarify your doubts personally.

How can I get updates about the new batch?

You can connect with Akash and get in the updates group of the TRADR MENTORSHIP PROGRAM.

What all is covered in the program?

You can see the entire course outline and download our Detailed PDF from the TRADR Mentorship Program section.

I know nothing about trading or the stock market, can I join?

The program is designed to train you from the very basics of the stock market and trading. It doesn't matter if you haven't heard of the stock market before or you are an intermediated trader or you are facing inconsistency in trading. This program is designed for all.

What is the duration of the trading?

For members in the city of Nagpur, MAH, the training is for two months, with LIVE classes on the trading floor at the TRADR office 3 times a week. For members out of Nagpur city, the training will be for 6 days straight, where your food and stay will be taken care of by the TRADR office.

What are the timings for the sessions?

For Nagpur Batch - 6 PM - 8 PM For Batch outside of Nagpur - 6 hours/Day with 3 breaks. NOTE - As there are only 5 members in every batch, we can alter the timings according to the group convenience.

What will I get in the subscription?

The subscription will contain high-quality signals for a month with proper direction, entry price, stop-loss price and profit booking prices.

How do I subscribe?

Just connect with our associates and download our app from Playstore or Appstore. You'll be added to TRADR group for updates and you'll get trades on the TRADR app.

What segment of signals we provide?

We currently provide signals for the FUTURES segment and will soon be adding INDEX OPTIONS and CRYPTO TRADES.

What is TRADR AI?

It is an account management system in which the trades in your account are taken by algorithms.

How to start with TRADR AI?

You need to connect with our TRADR associates, open an account with us and you are all set to start.

Is it safe? What if I lose all my money?

It is 100% safe and the account will be under your name. No one can touch your money without your permission so you can't lose all your money.

Can I see the trades in my account?

Yes, you can, because the account is 100% yours, you can log in with your broker and see the trades in your account. In addition, you'll get access to a live dashboard to view the performance of the AI on your account.

Do I need to log in every day to trade?

No, once you put your account on automation. you don't need to look at screens all day or even log in to your account.

How much risk is involved in investing with a robot or automation?

Automation reduces the risk. All the algorithms follow a strict risk management system so your account is never over-leveraged.
